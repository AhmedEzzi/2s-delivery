Privacy Policy – 2S Delivery

Last updated: [Add date, e.g. March 2025]

This privacy policy describes how the 2S Delivery app (“we”, “the app”) collects, uses, and stores information when you use the app as a delivery driver.

1. Data we collect

  • Account and login: Username and password (or other credentials) you use to sign in. This is used only to authenticate you with our backend (Odoo) and is not shared with third parties for marketing.
  • Location: When you tap “Update location” on an order, the app sends your device’s location (latitude and longitude) once. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Photos: Photos you take or attach to orders (e.g. proof of delivery) are sent to our servers and linked to that order.
  • Device and app usage: For push notifications we may use device identifiers (e.g. FCM token). We may also collect basic usage data to run and improve the app (e.g. errors, app version).

2. How we use this data

  • To let you log in and use the app (orders, sheets, delivery status).
  • To record the location you provide when updating an order and to show relevant information in the app.
  • To store and display photos attached to orders (e.g. for your employer or operations team).
  • To send you push notifications about orders or app updates.

3. Storage and sharing

  • Data is stored on our backend systems and, where used, on third‑party services (e.g. Odoo, Firebase/Google for authentication and push notifications). We do not sell your personal data.
  • We may share data only as needed to operate the service (e.g. with your employer or the organisation that manages the Odoo backend) or where required by law.

4. Your choices

You can decline permission for camera or location; some features (e.g. updating location or attaching photos) may then be unavailable. You can uninstall the app at any time to stop further collection.

5. Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, contact the organisation that provided you with access to the 2S Delivery app (e.g. your employer or the app administrator).
